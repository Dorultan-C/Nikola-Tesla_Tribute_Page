Nkola Tesla

Nikola Tesla was a scientist whose inventions include the Tesla coil, alternating-current (AC) electricity, and the discovery of the rotating magnetic field.

Nikola Tesla was an engineer and scientist and some of his inventions are:

  • Tesla is most well-known for his contributions in AC electricity and for the Tesla coil
  • He also created the "Tesla coil," which is still used in radio technology
  • He was also a pioneer in the discovery of radar technology
  • X-ray technology
  • Remote control and the rotating magnetic field
  • The basis of most AC machinery
